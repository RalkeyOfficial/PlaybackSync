03 · States

Three signals, one indicator.

The extension and room UI use three sync states. Each maps to a semantic color and a small dot — paired with text, never color alone.

State 01

Synced

All viewers in the room are within 250ms of the master timestamp. Steady-state. The status dot pulses gently.

State 02

Buffering

One or more viewers are catching up. Other peers wait at the last shared timestamp until everyone is ready.

State 03

Desynced

A viewer drifted beyond tolerance and could not auto-correct. Surface a manual resync action with a clear CTA.

05 · Usage

Keep the echo intact.

The mark's meaning lives in the offset and the ramp. Don't flatten, recolor, or compress the spacing — that's where the sync reading comes from.

✓ Do

Maintain the 20px offset

The horizontal step between triangles is what reads as motion converging. Keep it proportional at every scale. Below 32px, drop to the single-triangle favicon variant instead of compressing.

✗ Don't

Recolor outside the ramp

The cyan → sky → blue progression carries meaning. Don't make all three the same color, don't reverse the order, don't substitute unrelated hues. Use the monochrome variants when color isn't available.

✓ Do

Pair the wordmark exactly

"PlaybackSync" is one word with a tonal split: "Playback" in primary text color, "Sync" in --brand-blue. The split is the wordmark — never break it or change the colored half.

✗ Don't

Add effects or rotate

No drop shadows, glows, gradients on the triangles, or rotation. The mark is flat and oriented one way. Effects undermine the precision the brand stands for.

✓ Do

Give it air

Clear space around the mark equals the height of the front triangle's vertical edge. Don't crowd the mark with adjacent UI; let it breathe.

✗ Don't

Place on busy backgrounds

The translucent rear triangles need a clean surface to read. On photo or video backgrounds, drop a solid surface behind the mark — or use the favicon variant.
